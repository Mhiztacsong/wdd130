EXPERIENCE THE THRILL!

Whitewater Thrill Ride

Dive into the heart-pounding excitement of our Whitewater Thrill Ride. Navigate through Class IV and V rapids that challenge even the most seasoned rafters. Along the way, marvel at the stunning canyon walls and lush greenery that line the riverbanks. This full-day adventure includes a gourmet riverside lunch and plenty of opportunities to swim in the calmer sections of the river. Perfect for adrenaline junkies looking for their next big thrill!

Family Fun Float

Bring the whole family for a fun-filled rafting adventure that's safe and enjoyable for all ages. Our Family Fun Float features gentle Class I and II rapids, perfect for young children and first-time rafters. The half-day trip allows plenty of time for splashing, swimming, and enjoying the beautiful scenery. Our knowledgeable guides will share fascinating stories about the local wildlife and history. Snacks and drinks are provided to keep everyone energized and happy.

Scenic Wilderness Expedition

Immerse yourself in the untouched beauty of nature on our Scenic Wilderness Expedition. This two-day journey takes you through remote and pristine landscapes, offering breathtaking views and tranquil moments. Paddle through calm waters with occasional Class II and III rapids, suitable for all skill levels. Camp under the stars at our exclusive riverside campsite, where you'll enjoy delicious campfire meals and the soothing sounds of the river. Ideal for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful escape.

Sunset Serenity Float

Discover the magic of the river at dusk with our Sunset Serenity Float. This leisurely evening trip takes you through calm waters, perfect for relaxing and soaking in the stunning sunset views. As you drift downstream, you'll witness the sky transform with vibrant colors and reflections on the water. This trip is perfect for couples, friends, or anyone looking to unwind and enjoy nature's beauty. Light refreshments and a complimentary glass of wine are included.

Ultimate Adventure Challenge

Push your limits with our Ultimate Adventure Challenge, a three-day expedition designed for experienced rafters. Conquer some of the most intense Class IV and V rapids, navigate technical sections, and tackle powerful waves. This trip promises non-stop action and adrenaline, interspersed with moments of awe as you journey through dramatic gorges and waterfalls. Each night, relax at our rugged campsites with hearty meals and stories around the campfire. This is the ultimate test of skill and endurance for true adventure seekers.
