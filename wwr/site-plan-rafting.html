Audience

Our Water Rafting website is designed to cater to individuals of all ages who crave adventure, adrenaline, and unforgettable experiences on the water.

  • Who are the target customers? - Our target customers are pedominantly adventure enthusiasts aged between 18 and 45, with a passion for outdoor activities and a moderate to high disposable income. They are likely to be professionals or students who enjoy seeking thrill and excitement during their leisure time.
  • What do they want? - They want thrilling experiences and memorable adventures that push their limits and provide a sense of achievement. They seek adrenaline-pumping activities that allow them to connect with nature and create lasting memories with friends and family.
  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by accessing the water rafting website? - They expect to find comprehensive information about the rafting experiences offered, including difficulty levels, safety measures, equipment provided, and booking details. They will also seek information about the location, nearby accomodations, and any additional activities or amenities available.
  • What are their needs that aren't being met? - They may need reassurance about safety standards, guidance on choosing the right rafting trip based on their skill level, and personalized recommendations to enhance their overall experience.
  • How will my audience usually access my site? - Our audience will predominantly access the site through mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets, as they are likely to be on the go or planning their adventures while travelling. However, desktop and laptop access should also be optimized to cater to users researching from home or office environments.
